Our Causes

To arrange the monthly pension for widow women, poor childless old peoples and other needy humans who are financially weak.

Those poor girls who are to be married their parents are unable to get married due to poverty this organization arrange the marriage of those girls.

Those financially weak students who cannot get admission in school and college due to poverty. To arrange for this quality education.

Our Aim

Our main aim is to help people in our community. We do this by providing help and creating chances for a better life.
